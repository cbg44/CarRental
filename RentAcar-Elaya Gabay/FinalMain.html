About Us

EllaRental offers a wide variety of car leasing, and hourly rental programs – local car rental customers are picked up at no extra cost. In addition, the EllaRental Holdings Foundation has contributed more than $200 million to thousands of local non-profits focused on community improvement, education and environmental stewardship.

Founded in 1957,EllaRental is an internationally recognized brand with more than 6,500 neighborhood and airport locations in the United States, Canada, the U.K., Ireland, Germany, israel,France and Spain, as well as franchise locations throughout Europe. Through tremendous leadership and the entrepreneurial spirit of our employees, we have built the largest car rental brand in North America, known for exceptionally low rates and outstanding customer service.
